Note From The Founder

"Debate is a lot of things. It’s a way for someone shy to overcome their fear of public speaking, it’s a way for someone to fuel their passion to excellence, it’s a way for people with opposing views to come together, but most importantly, it helps us solve problems at a larger scale, as a country, united in our stance. Whether it’s a global crisis, or a regional adversity, debate gives us a voice among those who want to hear and be heard, and that is exactly what India Model United Nations is about. A platform, for debaters to come together, and make a positive impact as a whole, as a community. A voice, for Indians, India MUN."

- Naman Gupta,

Managing Director
